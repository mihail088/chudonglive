About us

Bringing people together, live

ChudongLive is a live-streaming social platform built on a simple idea: the best moments happen when people connect in real time.

Our mission

We started ChudongLive to make live, social entertainment feel close, spontaneous, and safe. Whether you're a creator going live to your community, a viewer cheering on a favorite host, or someone unwinding with a friendly AI companion, ChudongLive is built to make connection effortless.

We believe a healthy platform is a moderated one. That's why trust & safety isn't an afterthought — it's part of the product from day one, with AI-assisted filtering and a human review team working around the clock.

What we value

The principles behind ChudongLive

Safety first

We invest heavily in moderation and clear community guidelines so everyone can enjoy ChudongLive with confidence.

Honest pricing

Virtual credits are clearly priced with no hidden fees or surprise subscriptions. What you buy is what you get.

Respect for privacy

We collect only what we need to run the service and protect it carefully. Your conversations are yours.

Creator success

When creators thrive, the whole community wins. We build tools that help them reach and delight their audience.

Always improving

We listen to our community and ship improvements constantly, from better video to smarter discovery.

Global & inclusive

ChudongLive welcomes adults from around the world. Everyone deserves a friendly place to connect.
