Our mission我们的使命

Live streaming is demanding: you're a host, moderator, editor, and translator all at once. Chudong started with a simple belief — that AI should handle the repetitive parts so creators can be more present, more global, and more creative.直播很辛苦:你要同时是主持人、管理员、剪辑师和翻译。Chudong 源于一个简单的信念 —— AI 应当承担重复性的工作,让创作者更专注、更国际化、更有创造力。

From real-time captions that break language barriers to AI co-hosts that keep chats lively, every feature is designed to make a streamer's life easier without ever taking control away from them.从打破语言障碍的实时字幕,到让弹幕保持活跃的 AI 数字人,每一项功能都旨在让主播更省心,同时始终把掌控权留给主播本人。
