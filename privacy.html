Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Chudong ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our website and AI live-streaming assistant software (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, and password.
  • Billing information — your plan and billing contact. Card payments are handled by Stripe (see below).
  • Communications — messages you send to our support team.

Stream & content data

To provide features like captions, translation, and clips, the Service processes audio, video, and chat from your streams. This processing happens to deliver the feature you requested; we do not sell this content and do not use it to identify your viewers.

Payment information

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. Stripe collects your card details directly. We do not receive or store your full card number — only limited transaction data such as amount, date, last four digits, and status.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage & device data — IP address, device and browser type, feature usage, and diagnostics.
  • Cookies — used to keep you signed in, remember preferences (including your language choice), secure the Service, and understand usage. You can control cookies in your browser.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service and its AI features;
  • To process subscriptions and send receipts;
  • To provide customer support;
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud and abuse;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers — such as Stripe (payments), cloud hosting, and AI processing vendors, under confidentiality obligations and only to provide the Service;
  • Legal & safety — when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security;
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger or acquisition, subject to this Policy.

4. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Stream content processed for transient features (e.g., live captions) is not retained longer than necessary to deliver the feature, unless you save it (e.g., a highlight clip).

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email mihailradkevic2@gmail.com. You may also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), to protect your information. No method is completely secure, but we work continuously to protect your data.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have, we will delete it promptly.

8. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where we transfer data internationally, we take steps to protect it in line with this Policy and applicable law.

9. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time, posting the new version with an updated date and, where appropriate, additional notice.

10. Contact

Questions or requests? Contact mihailradkevic2@gmail.com or our Contact page.

本《隐私政策》说明 Chudong(下称"我们")在你使用我们的网站及 AI 直播辅助软件(下称"本服务")时,如何收集、使用、共享和保护你的个人信息。使用本服务即表示你同意本政策。

  • 账户信息 —— 如你的姓名、电子邮箱和密码。

  • 沟通信息 —— 你发送给客服团队的消息。

直播与内容数据

为提供字幕、翻译、剪辑等功能,本服务会处理你直播中的音频、视频和弹幕。该处理仅用于实现你所请求的功能;我们不会出售此类内容,也不会用其识别你的观众。

支付信息

订阅支付由 Stripe 处理,Stripe 直接收集你的卡片信息。我们不会接收或存储你的完整卡号,仅获得有限的交易数据,如金额、日期、卡号后四位和支付状态。

  • 提供、维护和改进本服务及其 AI 功能;

  • 履行法律义务。

我们不会出售你的个人信息,仅在以下情形下共享:

  • 服务提供商 —— 如 Stripe(支付)、云托管及 AI 处理供应商,均受保密义务约束,且仅为提供本服务;
  • 法律与安全 —— 法律要求时,或为保护权利、安全与合法权益时;
  • 业务转移 —— 涉及合并或收购时,且受本政策约束。

4. 数据保留

我们仅在为提供本服务、履行法律义务、解决争议和执行协议所必需的期限内保留个人信息。为即时功能(如实时字幕)所处理的直播内容,除非你主动保存(如高光片段),否则不会保留超过实现该功能所必需的时间。
