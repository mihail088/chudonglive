Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains when and how you can obtain a refund for purchases made on ChudongLive. It forms part of our Terms of Service. Please read it carefully before purchasing.

1. Nature of our products

ChudongLive sells virtual credits and digital goods (such as virtual gifts and premium features). These are non-tangible, digital items that are delivered to your account immediately upon successful payment and are considered used and consumed at that moment.

2. General policy — all sales final

Because credits and digital goods are delivered instantly and cannot be returned once delivered, all purchases are final and non-refundable, except where:

  • a refund is required by applicable law or consumer-protection regulation; or
  • the situation falls within one of the eligible cases described in Section 3.

By completing a purchase, you acknowledge that the digital content is supplied immediately and, to the extent permitted by law, you agree that you lose any statutory "cooling-off" right of withdrawal once delivery of the digital content begins.

3. When we will issue a refund

We want every transaction to be fair. We will review and, where appropriate, refund purchases in the following situations:

SituationEligible for refund?
You were charged but credits/digital goods were not delivered due to a technical errorYes — full refund or re-delivery
You were charged more than once for the same order (duplicate charge)Yes — duplicate amount refunded
Unauthorized transaction (e.g., your payment method was used without permission)Yes — after verification
An obvious billing or pricing error by usYes — difference or full amount refunded
Credits already spent on gifts or featuresNo (except where required by law)
Change of mind after deliveryNo (except where required by law)
Account suspended/terminated for violating our Terms or GuidelinesNo

4. How to request a refund

To request a refund, email mihailradkevic2@gmail.com within 14 days of the transaction with:

  1. the email address associated with your account;
  2. the date and amount of the purchase;
  3. your order or receipt number; and
  4. a brief description of the issue.

We aim to acknowledge refund requests within 1 business day and to resolve eligible requests within 5–10 business days.

5. How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method through our payment processor, Stripe. The time for the refund to appear on your statement depends on your bank or card issuer, typically 5–10 business days. Any credits associated with a refunded purchase will be removed from your account balance.

6. Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us first — we can usually resolve issues faster than a formal dispute. Filing a chargeback without contacting us may result in temporary suspension of your account while the dispute is investigated. We retain transaction records to respond to disputes.

7. Cancellations

Credit purchases are one-time and are not subscriptions, so there is nothing to cancel after a completed purchase. If we ever offer a recurring or subscription product in the future, you will be able to cancel it at any time before the next renewal from your account settings or by contacting support.

8. Contact

Questions about refunds or this policy? We're happy to help: mihailradkevic2@gmail.com or via our Contact page.
