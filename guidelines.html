Community Guidelines & Acceptable Use

Last updated: June 25, 2026

ChudongLive is a place to connect, create, and have fun — safely. These Community Guidelines, together with our Terms of Service, set the rules for everyone who uses ChudongLive. They apply to live streams, chat, profiles, AI interactions, and every other part of the Service. Breaking these rules can result in content removal, loss of features, or account termination.

1. Be 18 or older

ChudongLive is strictly for adults aged 18 and over. Accounts that belong to or appear to belong to minors will be removed. We do not permit any content that features, sexualizes, or endangers minors in any way — there is zero tolerance, and we report illegal material to the appropriate authorities.

2. Prohibited content and conduct

To keep ChudongLive welcoming and lawful, the following are not allowed anywhere on the Service:

  • Sexually explicit or pornographic content, nudity, or sexual solicitation, including in streams, chat, profiles, or AI prompts.
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexualization of minors — strictly prohibited and reported to authorities.
  • Violence and dangerous acts — graphic violence, threats, incitement, terrorism, or promotion of self-harm or suicide.
  • Harassment and hate — bullying, stalking, hate speech, or attacks based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.
  • Illegal activity — sale of drugs, weapons, stolen goods, counterfeits, or any other unlawful goods or services.
  • Fraud and scams — phishing, deceptive schemes, pyramid or "get-rich-quick" schemes, or impersonation.
  • Financial crime — money laundering, terrorist financing, or using credits or gifts to disguise, transmit, or convert funds.
  • Regulated and prohibited goods — gambling for real money, firearms, tobacco/vaping, prescription drugs, or other regulated items.
  • Intellectual-property infringement — sharing content you do not have the right to use.
  • Private information — sharing someone's personal or confidential information without consent ("doxxing").
  • Spam and manipulation — bots, fake engagement, mass unsolicited messages, or platform manipulation.
  • Malware and security abuse — distributing harmful code or attempting to compromise the Service or other users.

3. Virtual gifts & credits — fair use

Virtual gifts and credits exist to let you support creators and enjoy digital features. You must not use them to:

  • facilitate or disguise real-world payments for prohibited goods or services;
  • launder money or move funds between accounts; or
  • solicit or provide sexual or other prohibited content in exchange for gifts.

Credits have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for money. Any attempt to convert credits into cash outside of features we provide is prohibited.

4. AI interactions

Our AI companions follow the same rules. Do not attempt to use AI features to generate sexual content involving minors, illegal content, or content that violates these Guidelines. Misuse of AI features may result in account action.

5. Respect others

Treat everyone with respect. Disagreement is fine; harassment is not. Keep streams and chats appropriate for a general adult audience, and follow the reasonable instructions of creators in their own rooms.

6. Moderation & enforcement

We use a combination of automated detection and a human trust & safety team that operates 24/7. Depending on the severity and history of a violation, we may:

  • remove or restrict content;
  • issue a warning;
  • limit access to features such as streaming or gifting;
  • suspend or permanently terminate the account; and/or
  • report illegal activity to law enforcement.

7. Reporting

If you see content or behavior that breaks these rules, please report it using the in-app report tools or email mihailradkevic2@gmail.com. You can also block any user. We review reports promptly and take appropriate action. Reports are confidential.

8. Appeals

If you believe we made a mistake in moderating your content or account, you may appeal by contacting mihailradkevic2@gmail.com. We will review eligible appeals and respond.

9. Updates

We may update these Guidelines as our community grows and as we learn how to keep ChudongLive safe. Continued use of the Service after an update means you accept the revised Guidelines.

Thank you for helping keep ChudongLive a safe, welcoming place for everyone. Questions? Contact us.
