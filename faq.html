Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about credits, payments, refunds, and staying safe on ChudongLive.

Getting started

What is ChudongLive?

ChudongLive is a live-streaming social platform. You can host or watch live streams, chat with other members and AI companions in real time, and use virtual credits to send digital gifts and unlock premium features.

Do I need to pay to use ChudongLive?

No. Creating an account, watching streams, and basic chatting are free. Virtual credits are optional and only needed if you want to send gifts or unlock premium digital features.

How old do I have to be?

You must be at least 18 years old to create an account, use ChudongLive, or make any purchase. We may ask you to verify your age.

Credits & payments

What are ChudongLive Credits?

Credits are a prepaid virtual currency used only inside ChudongLive. You buy them with real money and spend them on digital goods such as virtual gifts and premium features. Credits have no cash value and cannot be redeemed or withdrawn for real money.

How do I buy credits?

Choose a package on the Pricing page and complete checkout. Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Credits are added to your wallet instantly after a successful payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and other methods supported by Stripe in your region. All payments are processed over an encrypted connection.

Will this charge appear on my statement?

Yes. Purchases appear on your card or bank statement as CHUDONGLIVE. You'll also receive an email receipt for every purchase.

Is this a subscription?

No. Credit packages are one-time purchases. You are only charged when you choose to buy a package — there are no recurring charges.

Refunds & cancellations

Can I get a refund?

Because credits and digital items are delivered instantly and consumed immediately, all purchases are generally final and non-refundable, except where required by law or in cases such as unauthorized or duplicate charges. If something went wrong with your purchase, contact us within 14 days and we'll review it. See the full Refund & Cancellation Policy.

I was charged but didn't receive my credits — what do I do?

We're sorry! This is rare. Email mihailradkevic2@gmail.com with your receipt and we'll credit your account or issue a refund for the affected purchase.

I see a charge I don't recognize.

Contact us right away at mihailradkevic2@gmail.com. If a charge was unauthorized, we'll investigate and refund eligible transactions promptly.

AI chat & safety

Are the AI companions real people?

No. AI companions are clearly labeled artificial-intelligence chatbots intended for entertainment and casual conversation. They are not real people and do not provide professional, medical, legal, or financial advice.

How do you keep the platform safe?

We combine AI-assisted content filtering with a 24/7 human trust & safety team. We prohibit illegal content, harassment, and sexually explicit material. You can report or block anyone in a couple of taps. See our Community Guidelines.

How is my data handled?

We collect only what we need to run the service and protect it carefully. We never sell your personal data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
