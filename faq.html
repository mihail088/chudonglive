Getting started入门

What is Chudong?Chudong 是什么?+
Chudong is AI assistant software for live streamers. It adds real-time captions and translation, an AI avatar co-host, smart chat replies, auto highlight clips, and analytics to your existing stream.Chudong 是一款面向主播的 AI 直播辅助软件,可为你现有的直播加入实时字幕与翻译、AI 数字人共播、智能弹幕回复、高光自动剪辑和数据分析等功能。

Plans & billing方案与计费

How does billing work?如何计费?+
Paid plans are subscriptions billed monthly or yearly through Stripe. You're charged automatically each period until you cancel.付费方案是按月或按年的订阅,通过 Stripe 收款。在你取消之前,每个周期会自动扣费。
What payment methods do you accept?支持哪些支付方式?+
Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and other methods supported by Stripe in your region. All payments are encrypted.主流信用卡和借记卡(Visa、Mastercard、American Express),以及 Stripe 在你所在地区支持的其他方式。所有支付均经过加密。
Will the charge appear on my statement?账单上会怎么显示?+
Yes — charges appear as CHUDONG, and you'll receive an email receipt for every payment.会的 —— 账单上显示为 CHUDONG,且每笔扣款都会发送邮件收据。
How do I cancel?如何取消订阅?+
Cancel anytime from your account settings. Your plan stays active until the end of the current billing period and won't renew after that.随时在账户设置中取消。取消后当前计费周期内仍可使用,之后不再续费。
